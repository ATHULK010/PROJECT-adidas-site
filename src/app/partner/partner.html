Zidane x Beckham

Two football legends, one iconic collaboration. Adidas brings together Zinedine Zidane and David Beckham — both global ambassadors and timeless representatives of the brand’s excellence on and off the field. This partnership celebrates their unmatched legacy and influence on football culture, fashion, and youth movements around the world.

Zidane, known for his elegance and intelligence on the pitch, famously wore the Predator Accelerator during his 1998 World Cup triumph. Beckham, celebrated for his precision and free-kicks, made the Predator Precision an icon. Adidas honored both players with special edition boots — Zidane's in gold and Beckham's in a red-and-white design — blending heritage with innovation.

adidas Originals and Wales Bonner Present Spring/Summer 2025

Adidas Originals and Wales Bonner return for their Spring/Summer 2025 collection, inspired by the energy of port towns where cultures meet. The collection blends city life with coastal charm. It features a mix of sporty and elegant pieces—like lightweight anoraks, matching shorts, tracksuits, hoodies, polos, t-shirts, and skirts. Accessories include a large suede bowling bag, a smaller leather version, and classic Three Stripes socks.

The footwear collection introduces a new Wales Bonner-designed silhouette along with two updated adidas classics. The WB Karintha is a completely new design with a sleek sole and two stylish versions—one with sequins and satin, the other in soft toffee suede with subtle stripes. The WB Superstar Hi updates the classic with a slimmer sole and suede material. The Adilette slide is reimagined with a leather footbed and a handwoven leather strap made in Brazil. The campaign highlights the blend of city energy and peaceful seaside vibes. Featuring footballer Raphinha and other fresh faces, it captures stories of balance between urban life and nature.

adidas x Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1

Adidas x Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 is a dynamic collaboration between one of the world’s leading sportswear brands and the championship-winning Formula 1 team. This partnership combines high-performance innovation from both worlds—merging adidas' expertise in athletic gear with the precision, speed, and engineering excellence of Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS. The collaboration features co-branded apparel and footwear, inspired by motorsport culture and designed for both performance and lifestyle wear. From aerodynamic-influenced designs to premium materials, the collection reflects the shared values of speed, style, and sustainability. It also reinforces adidas’ commitment to pushing boundaries in both sport and fashion.

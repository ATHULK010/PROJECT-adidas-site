Who We Are

Adidas is more than just a sportswear brand — it is a symbol of passion, performance, and progress. With a heritage rooted deeply in sport, we aim to inspire and enable people to push their boundaries, both on and off the field. At Adidas, we believe that through sport, we have the power to change lives — by encouraging movement, building confidence, and bringing people together. In India and across the globe, Adidas is recognized not only for high-performance gear but also as a cultural icon that bridges sport and lifestyle. Whether you're an athlete striving for greatness, a creator expressing your vision, or someone committed to personal wellness, Adidas is here to support your journey. We are constantly innovating — from breakthrough materials and technologies to sustainable practices that reduce our environmental footprint. Adidas is shaping the future by designing products and experiences that combine functionality with style, purpose with impact, and performance with responsibility.

Our Mission

At Adidas, our mission is to be the best sports brand in the world. We are driven by a clear purpose: through sport, we have the power to change lives. This belief inspires everything we do — from designing innovative products that help athletes perform at their best, to creating inclusive platforms that empower communities. Our mission goes beyond competition; it’s about making sport accessible, fostering creativity, and building a more equitable and sustainable future. We are committed to breaking barriers and pushing boundaries. Whether it’s through sustainable manufacturing, championing diversity, or supporting grassroots movements, our goal is to lead by example and make a lasting impact. Every step we take is guided by the desire to move the world forward — through sport, for people, and for the planet.
